Our Mission

To provide exceptional physical rehabilitation services in the community through our multidisciplinary approach, positive reinforcement and using specialized treatment approaches.

Our Vision

To be the leading choice for physical rehabilitation services in North York and surrounding communities.

Our Value

We are dedicated to providing you with individualized treatment progress to optimize your recovery, promote healing and decreasing your symptoms through a variety of specialized services.

Bayview Rehabilitation is a multidisciplinary rehabilitation clinic that serves North York, Thornhill, Richmond Hill and surrounding communities. The clinic has been in operation for over 25 years in its current location and is designated as a Community Physiotherapy Clinic (OHIP) by the Ministry of Health which will allow it to provide physiotherapy services to those who meet Ministry guided requirements. For those who do not qualify for this type of funding, we provide physiotherapy in addition to other rehabilitation services using various fee-for-service models.
