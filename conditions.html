Conditions and Expertise

The Back, Neck & Spine

Bayview Rehabilitation staff is certified in the McKenzie Method®. The McKenzie Method® is a comprehensive approach to the spine that involves a systematic progression of applied mechanical forces which utilizes physiological responses to improve motion and function while resolving pain.

Jaw and Facial Pain

Jaw (TMJ) Disorders and Facial Pain Using unique treatment approaches, our team is dedicated to helping patients with jaw (TMJ) disorders and facial pain.

Osteoporosis

Bayview Rehabilitation is a registered Osteo-Circuit Clinic. Osteo-Circuit is an evidence based assessment and supervised exercise program for individuals with osteoporosis and/or other chronic conditions.
